About Us

Founded in 2011, our company has built a strong reputation in the construction industry, specializing in major electric tower lines, civil engineering projects, and large-scale sound system installations. With a focus on delivering high-quality solutions, we have successfully completed numerous projects in various locations across Uttarakhand, including Haridwar, Rishikesh, Srinagar, Dehradun, Ranikhet, and many other regions.

Our projects are awarded through government tenders, and we pride ourselves on executing each one with dedication, consistency, and the highest standards of quality. We have consistently delivered projects on time, earning trust and recognition from our clients for our reliability and professionalism.

In recent years, a significant achievement for our company was the successful restoration of a hydro power plant in the Godi district of Champawat. This plant had been closed for over 25 years, with several attempts by other companies to restart it, all of which failed. However, our team took on the challenge, and by 2025, we successfully brought the powerhouse back into operation, making a substantial impact on the local energy infrastructure.

Our journey continues as we strive to contribute to the development of Uttarakhand and beyond, bringing cutting-edge solutions to the most challenging projects.
