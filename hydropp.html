About Hydropower Plant

History of Powerplant

The Champawat Powerhouse has been attempting to operate for the last 20 years, but every effort has unfortunately failed. Locals often recount an incident involving soldiers, where they allegedly met with an accident and were left without assistance. Some believe that the spirits of these soldiers still haunt the area, preventing the power plant from functioning properly.

Our company, however, faced significant challenges over the course of 4-5 years in an attempt to bring the plant to life. After extensive efforts, including conducting numerous grand rituals, prayers, and even restoring local temples, we finally succeeded in making the Champawat Powerhouse operational. This achievement is remarkable, as it is something that has eluded success for the past two decades.
